Learn to prove mathematical statements. Automatically verify them.

Waterproof can verify your proofs in real-time. Especially for those learning to write proofs, mistakes are bound to happen, and you probably don't have a mathematician nearby to help you at all times. Waterproof is always there to help you!

Novel learning method. Notebook style.

Computer verification of mathematical statements and theorems is not novel. But, teaching students to prove maths, and doing this using computer verification, definitely is novel. Generally, writing computer assisted proofs is more difficult than writing out proofs on paper, as these proofs can get very tedious and trivial at times. Waterproof provides a more accessible syntax and powerful tactics to help students learn to prove.
